## Analyst Notes

The identified findings were mapped to relevant MITRE ATT&CK techniques to demonstrate how cloud governance weaknesses may be leveraged by adversaries during reconnaissance, credential access, privilege escalation, and data access activities.

This mapping helps security teams align governance findings with threat intelligence and detection engineering activities.
